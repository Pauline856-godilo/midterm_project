Top Cybersecurity Tips to Stay Safe Online

Protect your digital identity with these essential security tips.

Use Strong Passwords

Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Avoid Phishing Scams

Never click on suspicious links or emails from unknown sources.

Enable 2FA

Add an extra layer of protection by using two-factor authentication.

Be Cautious on Public Wi-Fi

Use a VPN when connecting to public networks for better security.
